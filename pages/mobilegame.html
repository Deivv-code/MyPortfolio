My First Mobile Game


Description:
This game is a simple mobile inspired by the tutorials from Pitone Programmatore. Challenge your skills as you navigate through obstacles and dodge enemies to progress as far as you can. Enjoy the thrill of facing dangers while traversing the streets of Florence, set against a breathtaking gaming backdrop. Enjoy an intriguing gaming experience thanks to the music of yyyn0889.
